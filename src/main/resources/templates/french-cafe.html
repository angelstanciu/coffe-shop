Steps to Make It

1.Gather the ingredients:


- 1 part hot strong coffee, French roast;

- 1 part milk, steamed.


2.Into a large cup, pour equal parts of strong hot coffee and steamed milk, reserving the foam.

3.Stir.

4.Spoon reserved foam on top and serve hot.

5.If you're artistic, you might want to try your hand at using the steamed milk to create latte art, or simply give it a sprinkle of cinnamon.

6.Enjoy it! (the most important actually!)
